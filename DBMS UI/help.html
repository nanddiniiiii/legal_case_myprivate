Frequently Asked Questions

Our system uses advanced AI Language Models to convert your search query and all legal cases into numerical representations called 'vector embeddings'. It then finds cases whose embeddings are mathematically closest to your query's embedding, resulting in a search that understands context and meaning, not just keywords.

This allows you to search using natural language descriptions and find relevant cases even when they don't contain your exact search terms.

The similarity score (e.g., 92%) represents how contextually similar a case is to your search query, based on our AI's understanding. A higher score indicates a stronger semantic relationship. It helps you quickly identify the most relevant documents.

Score Guide:
  • 90-100%: Highly relevant matches
  • 80-89%: Very relevant matches
  • 70-79%: Moderately relevant matches
  • Below 70%: May be less relevant
